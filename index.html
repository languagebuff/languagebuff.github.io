Southside Auction Co. is Northeast Oklahoma's oldest auction house, founded in 1956. Public auctions are usually held on Thursday or Friday evenings, 4 to 6 times per month. Real estate auctions are normally held on Saturdays or Sundays. Click on the Upcoming Auctions tab above for the most up-to-date information on future auctions. Our sales our also advertised in the Tulsa World.

If you have something you believe has a significant value and would like us to consider taking it to auction, e-mail us a photo and details. We can meet you to view the item and give you an estimate of its value. We reduce our standard commission rates when we believe an item will bring a significant sales result when sold at public auction. We also consider lower rates on select real estate properties as well and, in some cases, higher referral fees.
