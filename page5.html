Charity & Fundraising Auctions

Since our company's founding, Southside Auction Company has been selected to perform live charity / benefit auctions for schools, churches, commercial businesses, private organizations, etc. Live charity auctions are a great way to raise money for a worthy cause, entertain guests, and have a little fun with an experienced auctioneer!

We never charge the charity organization for our services, and we do not request that any of our "spotters" (those assisting the auctioneer in catching bids) be paid to assist in our auctions.

Contact us for more information or questions about a charity or fundraising auction.
